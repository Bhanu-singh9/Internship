Mid Bottom

The sky was painted in shades of gold and crimson as the sun dipped below the horizon, casting long shadows across the quiet streets. A gentle breeze rustled the leaves, carrying with it the faint scent of jasmine from a nearby garden. Somewhere in the distance, a dog barked lazily, as if protesting the day’s end.
It was one of those rare evenings when time seemed to slow down,
inviting anyone who noticed to simply pause and breathe.
